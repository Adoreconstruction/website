"We build, you adore" — a promise we've kept since day one.

Adore Constructions (AC) was established in 2020 A.D. (2077 B.S.) with the prime objective of providing quality services in Civil and Electrical Engineering. With a broad vision and a deep commitment to excellence, we step forward every day toward the competitive world of modern construction.

We have grown from a small local firm to a trusted name across Province 2 Nepal, serving clients in Janakpur, Malangawa, and surrounding cities with in-house architects, structural engineers, MEP contractors, and interior designers — all under one roof.

Who We Are

Our Foundation

Our Vision
To build structures that people truly adore — considering cultural and economic values — and to become one of the best construction companies in Nepal, leading the nation towards a better future.
Our Mission
To provide the best professional services in a timely and promised manner, build effective systems with progressive development and strong work ethics, continuously innovate, and respect and represent ourselves in the local community.
Our Objective
Adopt modern techniques and technology, maintain community standards, create a human-centered culture to increase skills and dedication, work across diverse sectors, and respect moral values to produce collective effort for a better future.
Our Approach

What We Do

Three pillars of service that define every Adore project.

Design & Formulation
Structure Meets Aesthetics
We design structures to meet both requirements and sustainability, with a focus on aesthetics and long-lasting strength, providing guidance to clients through innovative engineering approaches.
Instrumentation & Monitoring
Safety at Every Stage
AC provides systems to monitor safety and sustainability across roads, buildings, and drainage. We coordinate between engineers and operators to identify risks, improve work methods, and ensure compliance.
Supervision of Work
Value-Driven Execution
